Pastry

Rich, decadent layers of noodles, white sauce and meatless spaghetti sauce that will surely capture hearts. It uses The Vegetarian Butcher NOMince which means you can satisfy their cravings with less guilt but still with that creamy, flavorful goodness!

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Tomato Paste
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Black Pepper
  • White Sugar

Recipe

  1. Add oil to the pot and apply medium heat.
  2. Saute onions, garlic, celery and carrots. Add dried oregano and continue to saute until fragrant.
  3. Add the prepared TVB no Minced. Saute until well incorporated
